DOM Cloud provides domain registration to some global and indonesian LTDs. Our domain registration is provided by [DigitalRegistra](https://digitalregistra.co.id), an Indonesian registrar service. Our official domain registar panel lives on [srb3600.srx-s.com](https://srb3600.srx-s.com). 

## Why Buying domain from us?

DOM Cloud Domain Registration provides simple integration with DOM Cloud Hosting. When you buy domain from us, the nameserver is already set to `nsp.domcloud.co` and `nss.domcloud.co`, which is linked [to our DNS service](./dns.mdx). Your domain should be active immediately after purchase and doing email confirmation after purchase.

DOM Cloud also provides a domain transfer service to transfer your domain from an existing registar. But be informed, you don't need to do this to enjoy custom domain in DOM Cloud &mdash; it's perfectly fine to buy domains from Cloudflare or Namecheap or anywhere else to use our custom domain, just [set the nameserver to us](./dns.mdx#switching-to-our-dns) and it will work the same.

## Available Top Level Domains

We sell `.com`, `.org`, `.net`, `.biz`, `.xyz`, `.site`, `.online`, `.tech`, `.info`, `.io`, `.pro`, `.shop`, `.live`, `.news`, `.agency` global TLDs. Please see the domain registration page for pricing.

We also sell indonesian ccTLD such as `.id`, `.web.id`, `.biz.id`, `.my.id`, `.co.id`, `.or.id`, `.ac.id`, `.sch.id`, `.ponpes.id`. Because additional document needed that tipically requires prove of citizenship, it's only offered if your payment currency is set to IDR. Please refer to [document requirements](https://www.digitalregistra.co.id/persyaratan-domain-id/) and [domain name registration policy](https://pandi.id/public/files/2022/3/1646293922-2-002-k-dnp-kebijakan-pendaftaran-nama-domain-ix-2019-pandi-publik-versi-8-0.pdf) for futher reading.

All domain pricing is yearly pricing, there's no monthly pricing available. 

Please note that buying domains is different from upgrading account. You tipically want to upgrade your account besides buying a domain.
